How to Brew Your Favorite Coffee

Espresso

Brew: 18-20g finely ground coffee, 25-30 seconds extraction, 1 oz shot.
Preparation: Serve immediately in a small cup, enjoy straight or use for cappuccino/latte.

Cappuccino

Brew: 1 shot of espresso (1 oz).
Preparation: Steam milk to thick foam, pour over espresso (1/3 espresso, 1/3 milk, 1/3 foam). Optional sprinkle cocoa or cinnamon on top.

Latte

Brew: 1 shot of espresso (1 oz).
Preparation: Add 6-8 oz steamed milk, top with thin layer of milk foam. Optional latte art.

Americano

Brew: 1 shot of espresso (1 oz).
Preparation: Add 6-8 oz hot water to espresso. Stir and serve in a regular coffee cup.

Mestizo

Brew: Strong local coffee beans, brewed using espresso machine or moka pot.
Preparation: Serve as a single strong shot or with a bit of sugar if preferred.

Roasted Coffee

Brew: Medium roast beans, grind for drip coffee or French press.
Preparation: Brew 1:15 coffee-to-water ratio. Enjoy hot or iced.

Drip Coffee

Brew: 20g medium ground coffee, 300 ml hot water, use a filter drip method.
Preparation: Pour slowly, enjoy clean, smooth coffee. Can add milk or sugar.
